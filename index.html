About

I'm a PhD student (39th cycle, 2023–2026) at the Politecnico di Torino, in the Department of Control and Computer Engineering (DAUIN) @ Vandal Lab. My research focuses on applying reinforcement learning to robotics, with a particular interest in reinforcement learning, sim-to-real methods, soft robots, robotic manipulation, and long-horizon manipulation tasks.
